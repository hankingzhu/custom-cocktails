# Classical Cocktails & Recipes

---

## 1. Old Fashioned

**Glass:** Rocks glass  
**Method:** Stir

| Ingredient | Amount |
|---|---|
| Bourbon or Rye Whiskey | 2 oz |
| Simple syrup | ¼ oz |
| Angostura bitters | 2 dashes |
| Orange peel | for garnish |

**Method:** Combine whiskey, simple syrup, and bitters in a rocks glass over a large ice cube. Stir gently. Express orange peel over the glass and use as garnish.

---

## 2. Negroni

**Glass:** Rocks glass  
**Method:** Stir

| Ingredient | Amount |
|---|---|
| Gin | 1 oz |
| Sweet vermouth | 1 oz |
| Campari | 1 oz |
| Orange peel | for garnish |

**Method:** Combine all ingredients in a mixing glass with ice. Stir until chilled. Strain into a rocks glass over ice. Garnish with an orange peel.

---

## 3. Martini

**Glass:** Martini glass  
**Method:** Stir

| Ingredient | Amount |
|---|---|
| Gin | 2½ oz |
| Dry vermouth | ½ oz |
| Lemon twist or olive | for garnish |

**Method:** Combine gin and vermouth in a mixing glass with ice. Stir until well chilled. Strain into a chilled martini glass. Garnish with a lemon twist or olive.

## 5. Daiquiri

**Glass:** Coupe  
**Method:** Shake

| Ingredient | Amount |
|---|---|
| White rum | 2 oz |
| Fresh lime juice | ¾ oz |
| Simple syrup | ¾ oz |

**Method:** Combine all ingredients in a shaker with ice. Shake vigorously. Double strain into a chilled coupe.

---

## 6. Margarita

**Glass:** Rocks glass or coupe  
**Method:** Shake

| Ingredient | Amount |
|---|---|
| Tequila (blanco) | 2 oz |
| Fresh lime juice | ¾ oz |
| Cointreau or triple sec | ¾ oz |
| Salt | for rim (optional) |
| Lime wheel | for garnish |

**Method:** Rim the glass with salt if desired. Combine all ingredients in a shaker with ice. Shake well and strain into glass over ice or up. Garnish with a lime wheel.

---

## 7. Whiskey Sour

**Glass:** Rocks glass  
**Method:** Shake

| Ingredient | Amount |
|---|---|
| Bourbon | 2 oz |
| Fresh lemon juice | ¾ oz |
| Simple syrup | ¾ oz |
| Egg white | 1 (optional) |
| Angostura bitters | for garnish |

**Method:** If using egg white, dry shake all ingredients first. Add ice, shake again. Strain into glass over ice. Garnish with a few drops of bitters.

---

## 8. Mojito

**Glass:** Highball glass  
**Method:** Build

| Ingredient | Amount |
|---|---|
| White rum | 2 oz |
| Fresh lime juice | 1 oz |
| Simple syrup | ¾ oz |
| Fresh mint leaves | 8–10 |
| Soda water | to top |
| Mint sprig | for garnish |

**Method:** Lightly muddle mint with lime juice and simple syrup in the glass. Add rum and fill with crushed ice. Top with soda water. Garnish with a mint sprig.

## 11. Aperol Spritz

**Glass:** Wine glass  
**Method:** Build

| Ingredient | Amount |
|---|---|
| Aperol | 3 oz |
| Prosecco | 3 oz |
| Soda water | splash |
| Orange slice | for garnish |

**Method:** Fill a large wine glass with ice. Add Aperol, then prosecco, then a splash of soda. Stir gently. Garnish with an orange slice.

## 12. Moscow Mule

**Glass:** Copper mug  
**Method:** Build

| Ingredient | Amount |
|---|---|
| Vodka | 2 oz |
| Fresh lime juice | ½ oz |
| Ginger beer | 4–5 oz |
| Lime wedge | for garnish |

**Method:** Fill a copper mug with ice. Add vodka and lime juice. Top with ginger beer and stir gently. Garnish with a lime wedge.

---

## 13. Tom Collins

**Glass:** Collins glass  
**Method:** Build/Shake

| Ingredient | Amount |
|---|---|
| Gin | 1½ oz |
| Fresh lemon juice | 1 oz |
| Simple syrup | ½ oz |
| Soda water | to top |
| Lemon slice & cherry | for garnish |

**Method:** Combine gin, lemon juice, and syrup in a shaker with ice. Shake briefly, strain into an ice-filled Collins glass. Top with soda water. Garnish with lemon and a cherry.

---

## 15. Dark & Stormy

**Glass:** Highball glass  
**Method:** Build

| Ingredient | Amount |
|---|---|
| Dark rum (Gosling's) | 2 oz |
| Ginger beer | 4–5 oz |
| Fresh lime juice | ½ oz |
| Lime wedge | for garnish |

**Method:** Fill a highball glass with ice. Add ginger beer and lime juice. Float dark rum on top. Garnish with a lime wedge.

*All measurements in US fluid ounces.*
